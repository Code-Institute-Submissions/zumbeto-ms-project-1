Everything you need for a safe and sound workout

Train with confidence

We’re committed to providing our gym members with a safe and affordable place to get a great workout. Being physically and mentally fit has never been more important and we are proud of the role that we are able to play.

Keeping a safe distance

We’ve created the space you need to work out while keeping a safe distance. The gym has also been rearranged and clearly marked out for you to use safely, so it is easy for everyone to follow. We also control the numbers in the gym at any given time so it never gets too busy.

Frequent cleaning

Our gyms will be deep cleaned every night with high-grade anti-viral disinfectant. The team will also clean every piece of kit and surface throughout the day, from dumbbells to treadmills and everything in between, so you can train with confidence.

Hand sanitising stations

We've provided hand sanitising stations around the gym so it's easy to regularly clean your hands during your visit. Keep yourself and others safe.

High-spec equipment

Use a wide variety of quality cardio equipment, weights and functional training space in your local club. We provide everything you may need for your workout.

We're open 24/7

Train whenever you like, 365 days a year – we’re here for you with a secure entry system.

Group exercise classes

Group classes are included in your membership, and there’s one for every ability and fitness goal.
